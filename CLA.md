## Why this exists

STC Core is **dual-licensed** (AGPL-3.0 + a commercial license — see
[`LICENSING.md`](LICENSING.md)). To be able to offer *your* contribution under
both licenses, the maintainer needs your permission. This CLA grants that
permission. You keep the copyright to your contribution; you simply also license
it to the project.

A plain Developer Certificate of Origin (DCO) sign-off is **not sufficient** for
a dual-licensed project, because it does not grant the right to relicense your
work under the commercial license.
