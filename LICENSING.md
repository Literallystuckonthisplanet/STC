You may use, study, modify, and redistribute STC Core **for free** — including
inside your own company — under the terms of the AGPL-3.0. The one obligation to
know: **if you modify STC Core and make it available to others over a network
(as a hosted service), you must release your modified source under the AGPL-3.0
as well.** For individuals, hobbyists, researchers, and other open-source
projects, the AGPL-3.0 is all you need and costs nothing.

## 2. Commercial license

A **commercial license** is available for cases where the AGPL-3.0's copyleft
does not fit — for example if you want to:

- embed STC Core (or a derivative) in a **closed-source / proprietary product**;
- offer a **hosted service** built on STC Core **without** releasing your
  modifications under the AGPL-3.0; or
- your organization's policy does not permit AGPL-licensed dependencies.

The commercial license grants those rights without the AGPL's copyleft
conditions. Draft terms are in [`COMMERCIAL-LICENSE.md`](COMMERCIAL-LICENSE.md).
